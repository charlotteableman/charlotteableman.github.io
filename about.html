Born and raised in Miami, Florida, I grew up with strong family links within the advertising industry. This has allowed me to build a perspective of the neccesary creative steps involved in bringing an idea to life. I began my education at the University of Miami with a focus in business, but soon felt a strong desire to bring my personal interest in digital design to the forefront of my studies. Following my switch into the School of Communications, I chose to major in Creative Advertising. Right away, my personal interest and motivation in my work grew exponentially. I soon after added Marketing and Interactive Media as minors as well. During my time at the U, I have grown to become a detail-oriented graphic designer, with a strong desire to contribute in a team-driven working environment.
